Computer Assisted Video Translator

A simple application that translates video upto 10 languages English, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Spanish, Portuguese, Malayalam, Japanese, French, and German. Upload your own file or paste youtube link to load them. Wait for one minute for processing.

or

All you need to do is upload the MP4 file and click Submit. Wait for the video to be processed. You can then click Play/Pause to listen to the translated video. The video will be saved in MP4 format.
