About CAVT

Welcome to our computer-assisted video translator! We are dedicated to breaking down language barriers and enabling effective communication across different cultures and languages. Our innovative system utilizes cutting-edge technologies to seamlessly translate videos from one language to another.

Whether you are sharing educational content, corporate videos, or personal stories, our computer-assisted video translator enables you to connect with diverse audiences around the world. Break down language barriers, foster cross-cultural understanding, and make a global impact with our video translation solution.
Our platform supports a wide range of languages English, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Spanish, Portuguese, Malayalam, Japanese, French, and German ; allowing you to translate your videos into multiple target languages simultaneously. Whether you are a content creator, educator, business professional, or individual, our video translator empowers you to reach a broader audience and expand your global impact.

How to translate

  1. Upload/paste youtube link of video: Click on the "Upload Video" button to select the video file you want to translate.
  2. Select source & target language: Choose the language which you want to translate your video. Our system supports up to 10 different languages for translation.
  3. Initiate translation: Click on the "Translate" button to start the translation process. Our system will transcribe the audio content of the video and then translate it into the selected target language.
  4. Download or share: Once the translated video is generated, you can download it to your device or share it directly from our platform with others.
  5. Get started
